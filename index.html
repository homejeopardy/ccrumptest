About

Catherine Crump is a Clinical Professor of Law at UC Berkeley, where she directs the Samuelson Law, Technology & Public Policy Clinic. Her work focuses on the intersection of civil liberties, technology, and government accountability. She is an expert in surveillance, privacy, and freedom of expression issues, and her research and advocacy have been widely recognized.

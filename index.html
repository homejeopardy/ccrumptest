Catherine Crump

Clinical Professor of Law
Director, Samuelson Law, Technology & Public Policy Clinic
UC Berkeley School of Law

Professor Crump teaches, writes, and litigates at the intersection of technology and civil liberties. The Samuelson Clinic supervises student work on litigation, policy, and community-engaged projects advancing transparency, privacy, and accountable government use of technology.

View Publications

Projects

  • Surveillance Oversight — Supporting oversight frameworks and public records work for government use of surveillance technologies.
  • Digital Rights Advocacy — Litigation and policy work focused on privacy, free expression, and government accountability.
  • Automated Decision-Making — Research and clinic projects addressing fairness, transparency, and due process in public-sector automated systems.

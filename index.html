Catherine Crump

Clinical Professor of Law, UC Berkeley

Catherine Crump is a Clinical Professor of Law at UC Berkeley and Director of the Samuelson Law, Technology & Public Policy Clinic. Her work focuses on issues at the intersection of civil liberties and emerging technologies, with a particular emphasis on government surveillance and privacy.

She teaches, writes, and advocates on matters involving privacy, transparency, and accountability in government and corporate practices.

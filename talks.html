The Small and Surprisingly Dangerous Detail the Police Track About You

In this TED Talk, delivered in 2014, Catherine Crump explores the risks posed by the routine collection of license plate data by law enforcement. She highlights how this seemingly mundane detail can reveal sensitive information about people's lives, raising profound questions about privacy, surveillance, and civil liberties.
